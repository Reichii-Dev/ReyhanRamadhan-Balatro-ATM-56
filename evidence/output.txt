=== CARD RUN GAME — Design Pattern Demo ===

[ Factory Pattern ]
Created: Flat Boost — Adds +50 flat points to score
Created: Double Boost — Doubles your current score (x2)
Created: Square Surge — Squares your score (score x score) — extremely powerful!

[ Strategy Pattern + Decorator Chain ]
Hand: [ 10 8 6 4 2 ]

-- No modifiers --
  Base score (Sum Score): 30
Final: 30

-- With [Flat Boost] then [Double Boost] --
  Base score (Sum Score): 30
  [Flat Boost] 30 -> 80
  [Double Boost] 80 -> 160
Final: 160

-- Strategy swap: High Card Score, with [Double Boost] --
  Base score (High Card Score): 10
  [Double Boost] 10 -> 20
Final: 20
